Life Chest is an application where you can encrypt your sensitive files and read them securely using a password, a pin code, biometrics and much more!
We offer a wide variety of file readers including:
- Plain text reader
- PDF viewer
- Images
- Audio reader (Slightly unstable with some file formats and large files)
- Video reader (Unstable with big files)

We also offer a large variety of ergonomic features such as:
- File exportation/importation
- Multiple types of views like grid and list views
- Folders inside chests
- Encryption with ChaCha20 for reading speed
